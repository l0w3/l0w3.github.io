---
title: "Using Genetic Algorithms to Optimize Energy Harvesting Relative to Budgets and Geolocation"
date: 2022-02-02
author: l0w3
---

In this competition, my friend Andrei and I created a working Genetic Algorithm to optimize clean energy harvesting based on a budget and a geolocation. We also developped a mobile app to showcase how it could be easily used by anyone to determine which renewable energy devices should they use to maximize the energy production without exceding the budget.

This project was presented on the international competition "High School Big Data Challenge", hosted by the Canadian Charity STEM Fellowship, where we were awarded the University of Calgary Hunter Hub: Entrepeneurial Thinking award, as well as the Let's Talk Science: Analytics Talent award. It was an incredible experience and was nice to share our project with experts on the field.

Our work can be found at ![The official website](https://live.stemfellowship.org/portfolio-items/2021-2022-high-school-big-data-challenge/). The manuscript is also uploaded on my ![GitHub](https://github.com/l0w3/STEMFellowship_Alex_Andrei/blob/main/AD_AR-1.pdf) as well as all the code used to create our algorithm.
